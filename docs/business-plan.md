# Business Plan — Working Draft

_Modryn Studio · April 2026_

---

## Goal

$100k/year ($8,333/month) in recurring revenue. Lifestyle/freedom model — minimal maintenance, no bosses, no ceiling on time or income from physical labor.

---

## The Model

Lifetime deal (LTD) as the bridge. Recurring revenue as the destination. The LTD funds content and buys time. The content builds the audience. The audience converts to MRR.

---

## The Sequence

**1. Pick a proven market**
Copy an idea that's been done before. Proven demand, not a hypothesis. Competitors already exist — that's the signal, not the problem.

**2. Determine MVP from competitor research**
Study what competitors' customers want most. Reviews, Reddit threads, complaints. Build only that. Ship fast.

**3. Launch a private LTD**
Price: $59–$100 one-time payment. No free accounts — paid users actually use the product. Distribute through Reddit, Facebook groups, X, and LTD communities.

**4. Start content immediately**
Landing pages, blog posts, competitor/alternative pages. Never too early. SEO compounds — start the clock now.

**5. Launch on AppSumo**
Goal: close the AppSumo LTD campaign with $100k. That money funds content production for 1–2 years.

**6. Final private LTD**
One last offer to the mailing list. "Never available again." Close it permanently.

**7. Build social proof**
Ask LTD customers for Trustpilot and G2 reviews. Scan Reddit for competitor questions. Answer honestly. Own the conversation.

**8. Transition to MRR**
By this point, recurring revenue should be starting. The LTD money bridges the gap until MRR sustains the business at the $8,333/month target.
